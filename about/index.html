About Me

My name is Richard Perez, and I am 17 years old. Born and raised in New York City.

From a very young age, I have always been interested in Computer Science. When I was 2 years old, I learned how to use a computer. I was 6, I learned how to build and take apart computers. Fast forward 10 years later, and my love for computers has never been stronger.

As of now, I've dabbled a bit in the field of website-making. I was even a part of my school's efforts to create a brand new modern website!

In the future, I plan on becoming a full-time software developer. I'm an intermediate coder in terms of web development, and I am now looking to develop my proficiency with Java.
